# Privacy Policy

## Catatan Keuangan App

**Effective Date:** 11 March 2026

### 1. Introduction

Catatan Keuangan app is a personal finance management application developed by Mr.Rahman. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

### 2. Data Collection

Our app stores all data locally on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal information to external servers.

**Data stored locally includes:**
- Transaction records (income/expense)
- Categories
- Wallets/Dompet
- Monthly budgets
- App settings

### 3. Data Usage

The data you enter in this app is used solely for:
- Personal finance tracking
- Generating reports (PDF export)
- Data backup and restore (JSON export)

### 4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share any of your personal data with third parties. All data remains on your device.

### 5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data stored locally on your device.

### 6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

### 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
