# Privacy Policy for DompetKu

**Last Updated: 1 May 2026**

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at DompetKu. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy applies to the DompetKu mobile application (the "App") and explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information you provide to us
DompetKu is designed as an **offline-first** personal finance application. All data you enter into the App, including financial transactions, budgets, categories, and account balances, is stored **locally on your device**.

### B. Device Permissions
To provide specific features, the App may request the following permissions:
- **Camera Access:** We request camera access to allow you to take photos of your receipts or bills to attach to your transactions.
- **Storage/Photo Library Access:** We request access to your storage or photo library so you can select existing images of receipts or documents to attach to your transactions, and for the purpose of creating and restoring database backups.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

The information and permissions we request are used solely to provide and improve the App's core functionalities:
- Images taken or selected from your gallery are saved locally on your device and are linked to your transactions for your personal reference.
- Database backups are generated and stored locally on your device (or your personal cloud storage if you explicitly share them) to prevent data loss.

**We do not transmit, upload, or store your financial data, photos, or any personal information on our servers. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.**

## 3. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend that you:
- Use a strong device passcode, PIN, or biometric lock (fingerprint/face unlock).
- Utilize the in-app biometric lock feature (if enabled).
- Keep your device's operating system up to date.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., for exporting reports). However, the App itself does not integrate third-party trackers, analytics, or advertising SDKs that collect your personal data without your consent.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
